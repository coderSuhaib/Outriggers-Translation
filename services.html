Services

Technical Translation Services

We specialize in translation services for the industrial manufacturers. Most of our translators have been translating in the field for the industrial sector for years. With these years is the deep understanding of machinery and the nuances on how they work. Many of these translators set terminology for some the largest manufacturers in the USA. Translation of large machines should only be left to those who understand the machinery. All translations are done by humans, not machines.

Desktop Publishing Services

Just like translation, layout work with foreign languages should be done by experienced professionals. Capitalization, hyphenation and punctuation rules vary from language to language. Characters with unique diacritical marks are common in Latin-based languages whereas non-Latin languages like Arabic, Russian and Chinese use different alphabets or character sets, and some read from right to left. Outriggers has a team of DTP specialists who can provide this formatting, working with your electronic files in nearly any type of publishing program.
