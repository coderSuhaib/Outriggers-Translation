Who Are We?

At Outriggers, you'll have a team of professionals who have been working on technical documentation for the construction manufacturing industry for more than twenty years.Focusing only on the industrial sector, our translators have achieved new depths of fluency in technical terminology. To encourage their literacy, Outriggers translates primarily for heavy machinery and construction equipment manufacturers. We may not readily know what the term Spectrophotometry means but the crane term outrigger is familiar.

Just as an outrigger is supports a crane, Outriggers translations lends their language expertise to support manufacturers' presence in the global market.
