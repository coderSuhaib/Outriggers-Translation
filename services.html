Services

Technical Translation Services

We love a good technical document. Whether that text is an operator manual, an electrical schematic, or your marketing product guide, Outriggers can support you. Years of experience translating complex material have made our linguists literate in machinery terminology and complex mechanical processes. Who better to translate your machine’s documentation than a company that focuses all their energy on learning more about industry? And we use Computer-Assisted Translation (CAT) to ensure terminology consistency and reduce cost by reusing previously-translated material.

Desktop Publishing Services

Just like translation, layout work with foreign languages should be performed by experienced professionals. Capitalization, hyphenation and punctuation rules vary from language to language. Characters with unique diacritical marks are common in Latin-based languages whereas non-Latin languages like Arabic, Russian and Chinese use different alphabets or character sets, and some read from right to left. Outriggers has a team of DTP specialists who can provide this formatting, working with your electronic files in nearly any type of publishing program.

Multimedia Translation Services

We offer various types of multimedia localization to help clients market their products’ content globally. We offer narration and media engineering of eLearning courses so clients can train their international professional engineers and mechanics as well as provide learning opportunities for customers.

Marketing and Website

Quality translation will influence buyers and impact brand image. Marketing and websites for industrial manufacturers require translators to both understand the specialized features of a particular product and have language skills to render an interesting interpretation.
